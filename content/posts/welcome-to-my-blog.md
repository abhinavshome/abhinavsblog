This blog is built with **Next.js static export**, which means I can write posts locally and publish a plain static website anywhere.

## What I want from my setup

- Fast pages
- Simple Markdown authoring
- Cheap or free hosting
- No database to maintain

That tradeoff fits a personal blog really well.
